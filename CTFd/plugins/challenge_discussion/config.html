How it works
  1. Students solve a challenge and click Writeup in the challenge modal.
  2. They write up their solution in Markdown — editable until you review it.
  3. You open the Review Queue, expand a submission, score each criterion, and submit.
  4. The student sees their scores and your feedback on their writeup page.
  5. All reviewed writeups (content + scores) become visible to every solver of that challenge.
